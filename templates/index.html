Our Mission

HandiCare is dedicated to providing accessible support services for people with disabilities. We connect individuals in need with volunteers who can offer assistance, creating a community of care and support.

How We Help

Immediate Assistance

Request help for daily tasks and receive assistance from our verified volunteers.

Volunteer Network

Join our community of volunteers and make a difference in someone's life.

Emergency Support

Quick access to emergency contacts and immediate help when you need it most.
